Synopsis

"The cameraman is never part of the scene."

Gio knows this rule by heart. At 28, he is a wedding videographer who captures everyone else's happy endings while his own remains a blur of regrets. He has mastered the art of being invisible—an extra in his own life, hiding behind a lens to avoid the pain of living.

But life rarely offers a second take.

When a mysterious encounter sends him eight years into the past, Gio is given a chance to "reshoot" his college days. The setting: 2018. The target: Lia, the one who got away.

Armed with the memories of a lonely future, he has one semester to rewrite the script. But he soon realizes that knowing the future doesn't make the present any easier. To change the ending, Gio must face his greatest fear: putting down the camera and finally stepping into the frame.
